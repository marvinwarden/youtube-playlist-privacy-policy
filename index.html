Privacy Policy for YouTube Search Playlist

Effective Date: November 16, 2025

1. Overview

YouTube Search Playlist is a Chrome extension that helps users save and revisit YouTube search queries as a playlist. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data Collection

  • The extension uses chrome.storage.local to store saved search queries.
  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or share personal information with external servers or third parties.

3. Data Usage

  • Saved queries are used only to provide the extension’s functionality (saving, deleting, and clearing searches).
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are integrated.
  • No cookies or external scripts are used.

4. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your search queries locally.
  • Tabs: To verify if you are on YouTube and to open saved searches in new tabs.
  • Host Permission (https://www.youtube.com/*): To interact with YouTube’s search page.

These permissions are strictly limited to the extension’s single purpose.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties.

6. Security

  • Data remains private on your device.
  • No remote code is executed.
  • Bootstrap Icons and other assets are bundled locally to avoid external dependencies.

7. User Control

  • You can delete individual saved searches or clear all data at any time using the extension’s interface.
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Any changes will be published with a new effective date.

9. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing page.
